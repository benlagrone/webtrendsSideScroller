Our Mobile Audience is Growing Year-by-year

Each year, since we started recording our traffic with analytics tools, the percentage of website visitors using a mobile device has grown by 85%. the percentage of mobile phone only traffic has grown by 93%.

Clearly, more and more users use their mobile devices for getting information from MDAnderson.org

The unexamined life is not worth living.

Omniture helps the marketing team collect and digest insights into audience behavior. They can look at user trends, see what works, what does not work, if their fixes are effective, quantify and justify their efforts.

As a development team, we must collect and use the same data on our audience patterns and uses of our web applications. Analytics reports help us identify a failure point before our clients experience an error. This is a powerful tool to use to lead the Patient Digital Experience.
